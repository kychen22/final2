COVID-19

With COVID-19 overtaking the world within only a few months, artists have been forced to make a living in isolation. It is no surprise that art and writing have quickly become geared towards the ongoing pandemic and its impacts.

Music

Artists all over the world have been releasing music in quarantine. While the style may not have changed, the outreach has definitely evolved. Read more about how touring has been affected.

Some groups have banded together (online) to put together remote performances, such as Harmonies of Pianists Everywhere (H.O.P.E.).

Pictured above: The charity single "Stuck with U" released by Justin Bieber and Ariana Grande, the music video of which is filled with videos filmed in fans' homes.

Art

A survey by Americans for the Arts found that 62 percent of artists were fully unemployed. Some artists are turning to digital art to continue reaching an audience. More about it here.
